Frequently Asked Questions

Address potential concerns or objections with simple, clear answers.

What age groups do you work with?

We work with players from grassroots level (ages 6+) to semi-professional (ages 18+). Our programs are specifically designed and adapted for different age groups and skill levels to ensure optimal development.

How long are the Barcelona development tours?

Our Barcelona development tours typically range from 10-14 days. Each tour includes intensive training sessions, friendly matches, video analysis, cultural experiences, and comprehensive player evaluations.

What makes your methodology unique?

Our methodology combines traditional futbol training with modern sports science including data analysis, GPS tracking, video analysis, sports psychology, nutrition planning, and recovery work - all delivered by UEFA Pro certified coaches.

Do you offer programs for female players?

Absolutely! We develop both male and female players with equal dedication and attention. Our programs are designed to meet the specific needs of all players regardless of gender, from grassroots to semi-professional levels.

What are the costs for your programs?

Program costs vary depending on the specific program, duration, and level. Please contact us for detailed pricing information. We offer payment plans and scholarships for qualified players based on need and merit.
